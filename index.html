Cadaques, Spain

Cadaques is a small town in the North East of Spain close to the border of France. It is the prettiest bestest place you will every visit. It's charming, beautiful old, and new. Fun fact, Salvador Dali is from here! you can visit his museum in Cadaques!

Places to eat:

  • Can Rafa
  • Blau Bar

Things to do:

  1. walk around and go into the beautiful galeries and beaches
  2. go visit the Dali museum
  3. go to the beach
